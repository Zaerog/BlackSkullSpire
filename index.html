Welcome to Dinner at Blackskull Spire, a simple JavaScript-based Text Adventure game, based on true story of a DnD 5e campaign.

Type Help anytime during your adventures to review controls.

Type Inventory anytime during your adventures to review all items you collected so far.

Type Roll Perception anytime to look around in the current room.

You are Droop, a Goblin Mercenary, proud member of the Blackskull Clan. You might not be the smartest Goblin around, but you are a combat veteran, always bringing the right tools needed for any fight - The only thing deadlier than your hand crossbow and rapier, is your lack of body hygiene.
And yet, probably your most amazing skill is an unnatural talent in cooking. Granted, people are usually taken aback by a Goblin serving food, but once they try your dishes, they are enchanted.

Tomorrow is your great chance to show off, it is the anniversary of conquering BlackSkull Spire, therefore Tork, the brutish half-orc Barbarian and your friend and mentor, has put you in charge to prepare dinner for everyone on this special day.

Thus, your story begins:

You wake up in your quarters around noon, at the second level of the spire.
Your goal is to find at least 1 main ingredient and 1 seasoning ingredient and prepare them at the cast-iron pot in the kitchen and then serve the food in the Dining Hall for a grandiose dinner that will once again remind everyone, that you are King Droop of the pot, conqueror of tastes, connoisseur of the spire.
